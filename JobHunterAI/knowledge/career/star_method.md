# Career Knowledge: The STAR Method

## STAR Structure
- **Situation**: Define the context, problem, or constraints.
- **Task**: Identify the specific engineering challenge and target goals.
- **Action**: Outline the action steps, tools, and technical designs used.
- **Result**: Quantify the impact (e.g. latency, PRs merged, percentage improvements).

## Evidence-Backed Metrics
Avoid qualitative assertions ("worked hard to build"). Instead, use verifiable numbers:
- Latency ceilings (e.g. "45ms average ceiling")
- Performance improvements (e.g. "15% latency reduction")
- Trace tags (e.g. "PR #142")
