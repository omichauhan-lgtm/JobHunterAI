# Outreach Strategies & Templates

## LinkedIn Connection Guidelines
- Keep notes under 300 characters.
- Skip phrases like "I'm excited to apply".
- Focus directly on technical fit or shared overlaps.

## CRM Progression Stages
- **Discovered**: Initial posting matched.
- **Applied**: Assets generated and submitted.
- **OA (Online Assessment)**: Coding evaluation.
- **Interview**: In progress conversation.
- **Offer**: Package received.
- **Rejected**: Pipeline terminated.
