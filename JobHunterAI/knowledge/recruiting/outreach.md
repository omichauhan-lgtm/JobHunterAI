# Outreach Strategies & Templates

## CRM Progression Stages
- **Discovered**: Initial posting ingested and matches threshold.
- **Applied**: Tailored resume and cover letter generated, submitted.
- **OA (Online Assessment)**: Coding evaluation received.
- **Interview**: Interactive interview loop initiated.
- **Offer**: Written package received.
- **Rejected**: Candidate pipeline terminated.

---

## ✉️ Founder & Engineering Lead Outreach (For startups <100 employees)

When applying to early-stage or YC startups, follow up on your submission by messaging the Founder or Engineering Manager directly:
1. **Be Concise**: Skip generic introductions.
2. **Lead with Evidence**: Call out NIT Warangal and your open-source work at YC-backed ADEN or OMI Gateway.
3. **Provide Assets**: Include direct links to your GitHub and portfolio.
4. **Offer a Technical Trial**: Explicitly volunteer to build a prototype or take a technical assessment to prove capability.

### Template 1: Connection Request (Under 300 characters limit)
> Hi [Name], just applied for the [Role] position. B.Tech from NIT Warangal, active open-source contributor (added prompt routing features at YC-backed ADEN), and built omi-gateway (45ms routing middleware). Here is my code: github.com/omichauhan-lgtm. Let me know if I can build a trial project!
