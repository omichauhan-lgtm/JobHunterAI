# 01 - Platform Vision & Core Philosophy

## Core Philosophy
JobHunterAI is a high-precision **Career Intelligence Platform** designed to **maximize the probability of getting high-quality remote offers**, rather than simply maximizing application volume. The system treats the career search like a high-precision sales pipeline, optimizing for response rates, interview conversion, and offer conversion.

---

## 🌌 Final Discovery Universe

The system monitors opportunities across **five core categories**:

### 1. Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)
Direct integration or parsing for companies hosting portals on:
- Greenhouse
- Lever
- Ashby
- SmartRecruiters
- Workable
- Teamtailor
- Recruitee
- BambooHR Careers
- Jobvite
- iCIMS

### 2. Remote Job Platforms
Aggregates and filters roles from:
- Wellfound
- Himalayas
- Otta
- We Work Remotely
- Remote.co
- Remotive
- Arc
- Turing
- FlexJobs

### 3. Startup Ecosystem
Monitors and prioritizes early-stage and scale-up opportunities:
- YC Companies
- AI Startups
- DevTools Startups
- SaaS Startups
- Infrastructure Startups
- FinTech Startups
- Data Platform Startups

### 4. Company Watchlist Pages
Maintains a watchlist of targeted target employers, monitoring their career subdomains for new engineering openings.

### 5. Recruiter & Networking Intelligence
Maintains listings of Recruiters, Engineering Managers, Talent Partners, and Founders, generating tailored outreach scripts for roles matching the candidate's skills.

---

## 🔄 The Discovery & Ingestion Pipeline

Every discovered opening progresses through a standardized pipeline before human submission:

```
Discover ➔ Normalize ➔ Deduplicate ➔ Enrich ➔ Company Intel ➔ Score & Rank ➔ Resume Gen ➔ ATS Optim. ➔ Truth Validation ➔ App Queue ➔ Human Review ➔ Submission ➔ Analytics
```

---

## 🧠 Additional Intelligence Modules

### 1. Market Intelligence
Tracks trending skills, remote hiring policy trends, regional salary bands, and companies expanding their engineering headcounts.

### 2. Skill Gap Analysis
Weekly analytical report detailing:
- Skills already possessed but under-emphasized in matches.
- Technologies repeatedly requested in JDs but missing in the candidate profile.
- Highest-impact learning recommendations (e.g. Kubernetes, AWS).

### 3. Recruiter CRM
Relationship tracking ledger mapping:
- Messages sent, response rates, follow-ups, and interview rounds.

### 4. Application Analytics
Measures resume template effectiveness, conversion rates by project combination, and response ratios by startup tier.
