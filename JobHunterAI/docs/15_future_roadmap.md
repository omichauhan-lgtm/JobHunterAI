# 15 - Future Roadmap

## SaaS Expansion Roadmap
JobHunterAI is designed to be multi-candidate ready, paving the way for future SaaS migration:

- **Multi-user Support**: Database tables contain `candidate_id` foreign keys to isolate candidate profile graphs.
- **Auto-apply Safety Policy**: To ensure compliance with job site terms, the system remains a document compiler rather than an automated application submission bot.
- **Portfolios and LinkedIn Optimizer**: Evolve the knowledge graph to compile and push updates directly to personal portfolio websites and LinkedIn profile sections.
