# 10 - Recruiter CRM & Outreach

## Outreach Sequences
Networking campaigns are structured as multi-stage sequences to build long-term relationships with recruiters and engineering leaders:

1. **LinkedIn Connect**: Connection note under 300 characters, referencing their stack and avoiding generic fluff.
2. **Follow-up Message**: Sent post-application, highlighting direct fit and linking project code.
3. **Interview Thank-you**: Message expressing appreciation for their time.

## Recruiter CRM Logs
The CRM records all interactions in `recruiter_crm`. Message history is saved as a structured JSON list, tracking sent dates, recruiter responses, and follow-up flags.
